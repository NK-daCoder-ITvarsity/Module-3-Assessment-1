Fried Chicken

Ingrediants

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Unskin the chicken and wash thoroughly.

  2. Step 2

    Take the BBQ spice and mix it with flour to produce a brownish texture

  3. Step 3

    Take Two Eggs and scramble them using a fork

  4. Step 4

    Take a Pot fill it with water and place it on the heated stove.

  5. Step 5

    once the pot is on the stove place the chicken inside of it to Cook at least for 1hr.

  6. Step 6

    after one hour has passed take the chicken and lay it in scrambled egg yoke.

  7. Step 7

    Roll the chicken within the egg, yolk until it is covered with it and place it inside of the barbaque spicy flour and coat the chicken.

  8. Step 8

    Put excela oil inside of a pan. and allow the oil to reach minimal boiling point. Once it has reached boiling point you can start puttting the coated chicken within the oil.

  9. Step 9

    The chicken will primarily take about 15min for a nice golden crisp chicken and you can take it out of the pan for enjoyment.

Time

Prep Time: 5min

Cooking Time: 1hrs 15mins

Total Time: 1hr 20min
