T-Bone Steak

Ingrediants

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  2. Step 2

    Stir together salt, paprika, black pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, coriander, and turmeric in a small bowl. Rub over steaks on all sides.

  3. Step 3

    Cook steaks on the preheated grill to the desired degree of doneness, 3 to 3 1/2 minutes per side for medium-rare. An instant- read thermometer inserted into the center of each steak should read 130 degrees F (54 degrees C).

Time

Prep Time: 10mins

Cooking Time: 10mins

Total Time: 20mins
