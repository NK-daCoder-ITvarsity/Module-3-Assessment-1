Tuna Sandwitch

Ingrediants

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Put all the Tuna Filling ingredients in a bowl. Mix well using a wooden spoon, breaking up the tuna so the filling becomes fairly smooth. The pickles and celery will take a beating too which is encouraged -> flavour melding!

  2. Step 2

    Divide Tuna Filling between 4 sandwiches (or more/less depending on bread size). I butter the bread and use 2 pieces of lettuce per sandwich. Enjoy!

Time

Prep Time: 5mins

Cooking Time: 7mins

Total Time: 12min
